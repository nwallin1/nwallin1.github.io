I study Computer Science as part of the Class of 2021 at Binghamton University.
The opportunity to study at the #1 Public University in New York has been an unimaginably positive experience on my life. My professors are all amazing people many of whom are performing the highest level's of research in the fields that they teach, Each of my professors has given me a unique experience that I will take with me for the rest of my life. Whether it be the knowledge they taught me, the interest they gave me in not only my primary field of study, but learning to be a well-rounded individual, the times they reached out to help me understand course content or even teach me non-course content, and of course the immense knowledge they gave me in so many aspects of the Computer Science field that has helped shape me into the programmer I am today. The community is a conglomeration of domestic and international populations, all bring their own unique personalities and experiences to the community. At Binghamton, I met my first friends from downstate New York, my first friends from multiple other States, and my first friends from multiple different countries including South Korea, China, and the United Kingdom.
